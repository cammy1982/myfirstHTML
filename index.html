About me

Well, first I'm a mom, a mom like any other that loves her son. I've been working in administrative jobs for years, just drifting along and never thinking about the future. Since the covid-19 outbreak I started to think about my future. I need to find a better career, something that will not be replaced by automation or robots any time soon. Programming is the way to go. So right now I'm starting with the cornerstones of the web, which are HTML and CSS.
